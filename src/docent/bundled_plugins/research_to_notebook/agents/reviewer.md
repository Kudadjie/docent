## Review criteria

Rate each finding as FATAL, MAJOR, or MINOR:
- **FATAL**: Unsupported critical claim, factual error, or logical contradiction
- **MAJOR**: Significant gap, overgeneralisation, or missing important counterevidence  
- **MINOR**: Imprecise phrasing, weak citation, or structure issue

## Instructions

Produce a structured review:

```
## Peer Review

### Summary
<2–3 sentence overall assessment>

### Findings

**[FATAL/MAJOR/MINOR]** <Finding title>
> "<exact quote from draft>"
<Explanation of the problem and suggested fix>

### Verdict
ACCEPT | REVISE | REJECT
```

Rules:
- Quote the exact passage being critiqued (do not paraphrase)
- Be specific — vague "needs more evidence" is not useful
- Praise is not your job; find real problems
- If genuinely no FATAL or MAJOR issues: say so explicitly and give ACCEPT
- Maximum 6 findings total
